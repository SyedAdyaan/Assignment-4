Admission Procedure !

There is no written test for pre-school. The admission test for primary
and secondary school is based on the following subjects and classes: KG & I - English,
Urdu, Mathematics and General Knowledge (oral)
STEPS :
1)Schedule for date of interview and the admission test is announced.
2)An oral and written test is conducted as per schedule.
3)A child must first clear the written test before qualifying for interview
(written test not applicable for pre-school).
4)The child and his/her parents are interviewed by the School Head
(and possibly also by attending staff members). 5)Should the child clear admission
test and interview, the admission fee invoice is to be submitted at the school
to complete the admission process.

The Evergreens Academic/Training Department works very closely with teachers for
a common objective: to improve the quality of teaching and facilitate learning
in every way. Our high quality, standardized curriculum has produced some
of the best results in the country. The Academic /Training Department
has a robust Professional Development Programme for teacher training
and another support function is to provide subject specific Term wise CRP
(Curriculum Resource Packs) Daily Lesson Plans (DLPs) and Assessment Packs to
teachers. DLPs allow teachers to focus on lesson implementation; this helps
in improving teaching standards by taking away the daily planning time and effort put
in by the teachers for the lesson planning. DLPs have been structured around
the Curriculum Resource Packs (CRPs) subjects and incorporate interactive
teaching and learning approaches. Curriculum Resource Packs comprise of a scheme of studies related to a centralized book list of private and government publications. CRPs are developed for two terms respectively. The teachers use these CRPs and DLPs to ensure effective teaching and learning in the classrooms.
